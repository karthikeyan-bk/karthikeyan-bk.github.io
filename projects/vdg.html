24/7 car buying and selling system

  • Tech Stack: Python, SQL, javascript, bootstrap, html, css

This project is aimed at developing a Web application that depicts online used Vehicle buying and selling vehicles through online on a particular company. Company can display the vehicles which is available for the sales through this application. Customer can register to this site and they can book and sell their vehicles by entering his login information. Administrator is main user of this system and he can add employees, and new vehicle details. Customers/Users are able to apply through online itself across the country.
